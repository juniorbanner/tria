1. Problem & Solution

Problem — Uzbekistan buyers struggle to compare prices, seller reliability, delivery times, and authenticity across local marketplaces (Uzum, OLX, Korzinka, ZoodMall, small shops). This causes wasted time, overpayment, and fraud risk.

Solution

Tria is a lightweight AI-backed advisor that:

  • Aggregates product listings across marketplaces
  • Ranks best deals by price, delivery time, and seller trust
  • Summarizes reviews with NLP and detects suspicious sellers
  • Offers an AI chat assistant to suggest alternatives

Buyer savings

Find the lowest total cost (price + delivery + risk) fast.

Seller trust score

AI-driven reputation and fraud risk signals.

Review summarization

Short pros/cons from hundreds of reviews.

Chat assistant

Instant alternatives and negotiating tips.

5. How we plan to solve it (Tech & AI)

Key steps, tools and AI usage:

  • Data: Scrape public marketplace listings and use public APIs where available. Store in PostgreSQL.
  • Backend: FastAPI or Node.js REST API, Docker for containers, cron/Airflow for scraping jobs.
  • Frontend: React (or a static Notion/Wix demo for submission). Simple search → results → detail.
  • ML / AI: Transformers / DistilBERT for review summarization, lightweight classifier for seller risk, ranking model combines price/delivery/trust.
  • Chat assistant: RAG approach: small vector DB + short-context LLM calls for search & recommendation.

Technical depth & innovation

Our use of AI focuses on actionable signals (trust, summary, ranking) rather than generic recommendations. The product is usable with minimal compute: heavy NLP tasks can run as batch jobs while ranking and search remain real-time.
